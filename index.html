My Biography

Oni, Zaccheaus Olalekan

Zaccheaus is a native of Omu-Aran, Kwara state. He attended his primary school at Concordial Faith International Nursery and Primary School, Omu-Aran, Kwara state. He proceeded to Amazing Grace College, Otun-Ekiti, Ekiti State, where he earned SSCE certificate. He bagged a First Class Degree from the department of Geology, Ekiti State University, Ado Ekiti. He participated in several geological conferences. He held several leadership positions during his undergraduate days because of his passion for excellent leadership. When at the helm of leadership, the administration recorded and completed numerous projects, few of them include: Provision of photocopier machine and printers for the department (first of its kind), Partitioning, furnishing and equipping of the office of Executives, and charging spaces for the students because of lack of electricity. He did his National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) in Ibadan, Oyo state, where he served at Nigerian Geological Survey Agency (NGSA). He is a serial volunteer. During his NYSC days, he volunteered with National Drug Law Enforcement Agency (NDLEA), where he preached and campaigned against drug abuse, he also volunteered with UNICEF, and NPC. He has ambition and passion to become problem solving Software Engineer. His long term goal is to be fully established in the field and help the younger generation get started while creating employment opportunities and wealth. To kickstart his software engineering journey, he was privileged to be among the successful applicants admitted into TIIDELAB prefellowship 3.0. He loves to read during his leisure time, football games and go on adventure.
